YoutubePls

  • Auto-skip Automatically click the "skip" button whenever it becomes available
  • Auto-close Click away the popup ads that appear while a video is playing
  • Mute ads Silence video ads
  • I'm still here When Youtube pauses the video and asks whether you want to continue watching, unpause the video and try to close the dialog. This feature currently does nothing - it will be updated soon.
  • Play next When the video ends, click "play next" instead of waiting for the timer to run out.
