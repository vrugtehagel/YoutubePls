YoutubePls

  • Auto-skip Automatically skip ads by clicking the "skip" button whenever it becomes available
  • Auto-close Click away the popup ads that appear while a video is playing
  • Mute ads Silence video ads
  • I'm still here Bypass the popup windows that ask whether you want to continue watching.
  • Play next When the video ends, click "play next" immediately instead of waiting for the timer to run out.
  • Picture-in-picture Show the picture-in-picture button in the toolbar of a video instead of the "show miniplayer" button.
  • Defer pause When pausing while an ad is playing, don't pause the ad but instead wait for it to finish before pausing.
  • Hide cookie dialog On first page visits, hide the cookie dialog. Does not consent to nor deny cookies, just hides the dialog. Mostly useful for incognito tabs, though the extension does need to be enabled for incognito (at chrome://extensions) for that to work.
